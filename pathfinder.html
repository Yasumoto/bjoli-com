I've been having a great time playing the tabletop roleplaying game, Pathfinder. If you're in the San Francisco Bay Area, there are a lot of great games being hosted by the Bay Area Pathfinder Society. In particular, Endgame in Oakland hosts events on Monday evenings, and Gamescape in San Francisco hosts a game day each month.

I was also published in Wayfinder #13 with an article describing two characters, Captain Almira Perine and Captain Liald Gaspair!
