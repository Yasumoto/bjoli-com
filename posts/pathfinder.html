I love to step away from the computer to play the tabletop roleplaying game, Pathfinder.

Bay Area Pathfinder Society

I'm a Venture Lieutenant for San Francisco, which means I help organize events and conventions, in addition to running our Facebook page. If you're in the San Francisco Bay Area, there are a lot of great games hosted by the Bay Area Pathfinder Society! In particular, Endgame in Oakland hosts events on Monday evenings, and Gamescape in San Francisco hosts a Saturday game day each month.

Publications

Zenith Games recently published The Colossal Creatures Bestiary which contains a massive Raccoon-like creature I designed called a Procyon! It was a great experience to work with that group, and it's available on the Paizo webstore if you'd like to take a look!

The fine folks behind Wayfinder #13 also published my description of two characters based in the city of Caliphas! Spanning two pages and 1500 words, you can read about both Captain Almira Perine and Captain Liald Gaspair! The character art is by the amazing Bob Greyvenstein.
